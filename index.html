Presentation

Hello! Welcome!


I studied at the Escuela Argentina de Modas (EAM). And since I was little my grandmother taught me to sew, I studied systems but design always accompanied me.Today I can make the clothes you want to suit your size and color: T-shirts, Pants, Jumpsuits, Scarves, Shirts, Bracelets and Jackets, the Boca or River set (there is no "crack" in this place ) and your set croatian. Also, you can take your own mold(s) .
My wish for the future is to be able to open workshops to teach women who want a better future and allow them to be independent.
