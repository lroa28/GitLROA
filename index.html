Presentation

Since I was little my grandmother taught me to sew, I studied systems but design always accompanied me. Today I can make the clothes you want to suit your size and color: T-shirts, Pants, Jumpsuits, Scarves, Shirts, Knitted Collars, Bracelets and Jackets, the Boca or River set (there is no "crack" in this place) and your set Croatian. Also, you can take your own mold (s). My wish for the future is to be able to open workshops to teach women who want a better future and allow them to be independent.
